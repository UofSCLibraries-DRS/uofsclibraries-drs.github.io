USC Libraries - DRS Resources for Data Literacy

Introduction

This is the initiative of Digital Research Services (DRS), University Libraries at University of South Carolina to provide AI and Data Science resources to its students, faculty, and other academic stakeholders.

We are committed to providing a safe and responsible environment for the use of generative AI tools. We encourage our users to be aware of the ethical implications and to use these tools responsibly.

We also provide resources for citing generative AI tools in your research and projects. Please refer to our guidelines for more information.
